My name is Alex Farmer, a student inventor studying at Flinders University in SA, Australia. I specialize in Electrical and Electronics engineering, specifically in IoT remote sensing solutions. I am a member of the STEMSEL Foundation Australia, a non-profit organization promoting global STEM innovation using runlinc easy coding to develop student projects involving IoT.

Id love to chat if youre interested in what I do! contact me here!

Im likely doing something awesome like inventing something new, driving a solar powered car, or competing in a sailing race!

After years of interest in STEM and technology, I finished year 12 and graduated straight into my dream engineering degree at Flinders University at Tonsley, a world class engineering and innovation facility with amazing teaching staff. I also regularly work with STEMSEL foundation to realize my invention ideas and make them a reality.

Love for STEM and Technology

Since I was very young, I have had a natural interest in everything that flashes, beeps. and moves. This led me to chase my passion and discover myself through creation and invention. I joined the STEMSEL inventors club at the age of just 11 years old after discovering them at the Royal Adelaide Show, which I still compete in to this day.

My Vision - Driven by Passion, Delivered by Excellence

I believe that the true innovators of this world are not driven by the prospect of money, intellectuality, or business. I strive to intertwine passion for STEM, invention and technology in every step of every project. Innovation should be Driven by Passion for design, and Delivered by Excellence of the mind.

Other Interests

As well as a love for engineering, I love to enjoy other aspects of life in my downtime. My personal hobbies include playing the guitar, racing sailing dinghies, and teaching as a sailing instructor. Having a balance between business and personal life is key to making the most out of what the world has to offer, as well as keeping productivity and spirits high!
